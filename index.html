Welcome to Marbsailing
What is Marbsailing?
Marbsailing is a small business located in the city Marbella, Malaga province, Spain, which has, for the last thirty years, turned young men and women with no prior sailing experience into proficient sailors. Monthly prices for sailing courses with intermediate-to-large ships range from 50€ to 60€ depending on the season and client demand.

Our monitors are experienced and have experience with commpetitive sailing, as most have participated in the 2017 Franco-British Sotogrande Regata or the 2019 Regata Intercontinental Marbella-Ceuta. You will find that there are no better-prepared Sailing courses in the south of Spain.

This website is run and managed by the sub-director of our dynamic-Instruction department. For further inquiries regarding classes and availability, please refer to some of the the links and contact methods mentioned within our 'About Us' page.

We hope to see you sailing with us soon.
